company

Ferrari SpA is an Italian sports car manufacturer based in Maranello and Modena , Italy. The company was founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929 as Scuderia Ferrari and in 1949 as Ferrari SpA. Throughout its history, Ferrari has achieved its greatest success in motor racing, particularly in Formula One . In 1969 , during financial struggles, Enzo Ferrari sold the company's sports car division to Fiat in order to secure financial support.
